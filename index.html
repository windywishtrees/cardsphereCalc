CS Transaction Rake:
1% of every card traded is taken by CardSphere as a tranasction fee. That means for every $1 in value, CardSphere takes $0.01.

CS Cashout Rake:
When cashing out via PayPal, CardSphere takes $10 or 10% (whichever is greater) of the total cashout amount. If you cashout for $200, CardSphere will take $20. This calculator assumes that you will always be cashing out for at least $100 and as such, removes 10% of the card's value after the transaction rake value but before shipping (since you will always pay those costs but it isn't accounted for within CardSphere itself).
